UNITED REALMS

The UR was created by Rolen Halamar 10 years after the realm wide introduction of space travel,
the galaxy was plagued by war and destruction, peace and order needed to be enforced,
the Elves, Dwarfs, Humans, Fairies and Dragonborn were the first to come together in the name of peace,
in the hundreds of years since many more races have joined the UR.

Your job will be to assist in spreading peace and prosperity to any and all remaining systems,
your missions may include:

Escorting personnel
Transporting precious cargo
Dealing with local disruptions
Scouting out undiscovered planets

Upon completion of training you will all be given a Coms-pad, UR Crew Credit Link and a E-40 Blaster

--WARNING--
Any artifacts discovered related to the annihilator must be delivered to the nearest research station,
prolonged contact with these artifacts may cause physical harm.
